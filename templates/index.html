About Us

This website, a collaboration by three Sfax Engineering School students - Oussama Slimani, Siwar Gouirah, and Iheb Charfeddine, serves a crucial purpose: aiding doctors in swiftly identifying brain tumors. Its functionality enables the seamless upload and analysis of MRI images. The primary objective is to streamline the diagnostic process, facilitating quicker and more accurate detection of brain abnormalities. The platform aims to enhance medical professionals' efficiency, ensuring timely intervention for patients with potential neurological issues.
The main objectives are:

  • Quickly spots brain tumors using easy MRI image uploads.
  • Helps doctors act faster in neurological cases.
  • Speeds up brain tumor identification through simple MRI image uploads.

Effortlessly detects brain tumors with a user-friendly interface for easy MRI image uploads.

  • 01 Upload the images
  • 02Receive a message indicating whether the uploaded image shows a brain tumor or not
  • 03If the message indicates a brain tumor, there is an option to perform segmentation on the images.
